Privacy Policy for YAPP

Last updated: March 12, 2026

YAPP is an augmentative and alternative communication (AAC) application designed to help users communicate using device-assisted interaction methods, including features that may rely on Apple TrueDepth and camera-based input.

Information We Collect

YAPP may use certain device data and app-generated data only as needed to provide core app functionality. Depending on the features used, this may include:

  • Camera and/or TrueDepth input used for gaze, face, head movement, or interaction detection
  • User-created communication content, preferences, settings, or customizations saved by the user
  • Basic device or diagnostic information needed to maintain app performance and reliability

TrueDepth and Face-Related Data

YAPP may use Apple’s TrueDepth camera system and related face-mapping or motion input solely to enable accessibility and communication features inside the app.

This data is used only to detect user interaction, improve accessibility controls, and support communication workflows within YAPP.

YAPP does not use TrueDepth or face-related data for advertising, marketing, profiling, or identity recognition.

How Information Is Used

Any information used by YAPP is used only for the following purposes:

  • Providing app functionality
  • Supporting accessibility and communication features
  • Saving user settings or preferences
  • Improving app stability, reliability, and performance

Data Storage

Face-tracking, TrueDepth, and interaction input used by YAPP is processed only as needed for app functionality.

Unless explicitly stated in a feature inside the app, YAPP does not permanently store raw TrueDepth data, raw facial maps, or raw camera recordings for external use.

User-created content or preferences may be stored locally on the user’s device or in the user’s selected storage environment only as needed for app functionality.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data.

We do not share TrueDepth data or face-related accessibility input with third parties for advertising or data brokerage purposes.

Information may only be shared if required to operate app infrastructure, comply with law, or protect the safety, rights, or property of users or the developer.

Third-Party Services

YAPP may rely on Apple platform services or other technical service providers to support app operation, hosting, analytics, crash diagnostics, or performance monitoring. These providers only receive the minimum information necessary to perform their services, if used.

Retention of Data

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to operate the app, provide requested functionality, maintain saved user preferences, or comply with legal obligations.

If certain content is stored by the user inside the app, that content may remain available until the user deletes it or removes the app.

User Choice, Consent, and Revocation

Users may stop camera or TrueDepth access at any time by changing device permissions in iPhone or iPad Settings.

Users may also stop using features that rely on face or gaze interaction at any time.

If the app stores user-created content or preferences, users may remove that content within the app, where available, or by deleting the app from the device.

Children’s Privacy

YAPP is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate authorization where required. If you believe information was provided in error, please contact us so we can review and address the issue.

Security

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect data and reduce unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
