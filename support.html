YAPP Support

If you need help using YAPP, have technical questions, or want to report a problem, please contact support.

Support Contact

Email: courtneyprater@systemsunlimitedllc.com

What to Include in Your Message

To help us resolve your issue faster, please include:

  • Your device model
  • Your iOS or iPadOS version
  • Your YAPP app version
  • A short description of the issue
  • Any steps that caused the issue

Response Time

We aim to respond within 2 business days.

Accessibility Support

If you are experiencing problems with gaze interaction, face-tracking-related controls, or accessibility setup, include that in your message so support can better assist you.

Feedback

We welcome bug reports, accessibility suggestions, and feature feedback that can improve the YAPP experience.
